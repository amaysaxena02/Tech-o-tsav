Tech-o-utsav (National Level Technical Festival) -2024 : ( 13 & 14 December, 2024 )
Organised by Department of Information Technology & Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering Acropolis Institute of Technology and Research, Indore
About Tech-o-tsav, 2024 “Tech-o-tsav,” a National Level Technical Festival, will be held on 13-14 December, 2024 featuring diverse events to enhance technical skills and creativity. Highlights include Innovate Expo, Concept Showcase, Code the Future, Design the Web!, Visual Vista Showcase, Code Craft Challenges, AI-Fusion: Where Ideas Evolve into Reality!, Mind Master Quizzard, Electronic Junkyard, Robo Race, High-Speed Line Follower Robot, Electronic Gadget Design, and PCB Designing.
Pre-event workshops will be organised by experts.


InnovateExpo: (interdisciplinary Project Exhibition) InnovateExpo is an interdisciplinary technical project competition designed to ignite creativity and foster innovation. Bringing together bright minds from diverse fields, it serves as a platform for participants to showcase groundbreaking ideas, collaborate across disciplines, and address real-world challenges. InnovateExpo celebrates ingenuity, encouraging solutions that push the boundaries of technology and innovation.

PCB Designing Printed Circuit Board are essential part of an electronic connections to develop a mechanical support. Conductive tracks, pads and other features etched from copper sheets laminated onto a non-conductive substrate to connect capacitors, resistors or active devices which are generally soldered on the Printed Circuit Board. Through PCB design competition the students will be able to design PCB in software through EAGLE, demonstrate practical knowledge of fabrication of PCB and will be capable to make compact design and prototyping.
